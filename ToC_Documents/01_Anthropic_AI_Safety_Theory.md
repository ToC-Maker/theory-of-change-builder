# Anthropic's Theory of Change for AI Safety - Full Content

## Executive Summary
Anthropic anticipates potentially transformative AI development within the next decade and believes AI capabilities will grow exponentially due to increased computational power. Their theory of change centers on empirically-driven research and multi-scenario planning to ensure safe AI development.

## Theory of Change Framework

### Inputs (Resources & Activities)
- **Research Funding**: Significant investment in AI safety research across multiple domains
- **Technical Expertise**: AI safety researchers, machine learning experts, interpretability specialists
- **Computational Resources**: Large-scale compute infrastructure for testing and evaluation
- **Multi-scenario Planning**: Strategic frameworks covering optimistic, intermediate, and pessimistic AI development outcomes
- **Empirical Research Activities**: Direct experimentation and observation of AI system behaviors

### Outputs (Direct Deliverables)
- **Research Publications**: Technical papers on AI alignment, interpretability, and safety methodologies
- **Safety Frameworks**: Responsible Scaling Policy (RSP) and AI Safety Levels (ASL) frameworks
- **Technical Tools**: Constitutional AI techniques, mechanistic interpretability methods, scalable oversight approaches
- **Testing Protocols**: Methods for identifying AI system safety levels and potential failure modes
- **Industry Guidelines**: Best practices for safe AI development and deployment

### Outcomes (System Changes)
- **Improved AI Safety Practices**: Industry adoption of safety frameworks and evaluation methods
- **Better Understanding**: Enhanced knowledge of AI system behaviors, generalization patterns, and alignment challenges
- **Risk Mitigation**: Reduced probability of harmful AI outcomes through proactive safety measures
- **Preparedness**: Industry and society better equipped to handle transformative AI development
- **Coordination**: Improved collaboration between AI developers on safety standards

### Ultimate Goal
Safe development of transformative AI systems that remain beneficial and aligned with human values, even as capabilities rapidly advance. Anthropic aims to develop AI that can help solve complex global problems while avoiding catastrophic risks.

## Strategic Approach

### Three Research Categories:
1. **Capabilities Research**: Advancing AI system performance and functionality
2. **Alignment Capabilities Research**: Developing techniques for making AI systems safer and more aligned
3. **Alignment Science Research**: Fundamental research into AI behavior, interpretability, and control

### Key Research Priorities:
- **Mechanistic Interpretability**: Understanding how AI systems work internally
- **Scalable Oversight**: Methods for monitoring and controlling advanced AI systems
- **Process-Oriented Learning**: Training AI to use reliable reasoning processes
- **Constitutional AI**: Teaching AI systems to follow principles and values
- **Testing Failure Modes**: Identifying potential risks before they become critical

### Portfolio Approach Philosophy:
Anthropic employs a "portfolio approach" that hedges across different potential AI development scenarios, ensuring their research remains valuable whether AI progress is fast or slow, whether alignment challenges are easy or hard, and whether other actors prioritize safety or not.

This comprehensive theory of change emphasizes adaptive, evidence-based research that can respond to uncertainty while maintaining focus on the ultimate goal of beneficial, controllable AI systems.
