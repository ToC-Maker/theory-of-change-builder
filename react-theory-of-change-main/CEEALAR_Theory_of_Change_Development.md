## 9. Chain Health Check

**Co-Pilot (9a):** Let's multiply probabilities from inputs to End Goal:

**Overall chain probability**: 80% × 90% × 75% × 70% × 65% = **24.6%**

🟠 This puts you in the moderate range (25-50% would be 🟠, but you're just below at 24.6%, so borderline 🔴).

The math suggests that for every resident you support, roughly 1 in 4 will achieve the full End Goal of developing skills, knowledge, and networks for meaningful EA career advancement.

**Options:**
1. Strengthen weak links (lowest probability: networks → careers at 65%)
2. Split the chain (maybe track intermediate successes)  
3. Accept the risk (24.6% success rate might be acceptable given high impact)

**Greg:** Actually, I think 24.6% might be conservative. Many residents get significant benefit even if they don't achieve the full End Goal. But the weakest link is definitely career advancement - that's where external factors matter most. Let me revise that to 70%, which gives us 29% overall - solidly in moderate range.

**Co-Pilot (9b):** Good adjustment. 29% overall probability locked. 🟠 Moderate confidence level.

**Greg:** Confirmed.
